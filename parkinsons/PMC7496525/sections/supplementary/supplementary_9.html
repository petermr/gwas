Supplementary Fig. 5 Haplotype group correlation analysis reveals a significant relationship between genotype and PD age of onset or diagnosis. Regression of haplotype groups against PD phenotypes identified a significant correlation between haplotype and two phenotypes: symptom onset age and diagnosis age (both highly correlated to each other; r = 0.18, p < 0.01). NA = missing data, “X” = a non‐significant correlation.
